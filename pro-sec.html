Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the Professional Pass?

The Professional Pass includes access to all featured sessions, exclusive networking opportunities, participation in VIP Executive Roundtables, access to the Private LinkedIn Group, downloadable resources, post-event materials, and a LinkedIn-Certified Participation Badge.

How do I access the Private LinkedIn Group?

Once you register for the Professional Pass, you will receive an invitation to join our curated Private LinkedIn Group. This community is designed for professionals to engage with peers, influencers, and speakers throughout the event and beyond.

Are there any discounts available?

Yes! We offer early-bird pricing for those who register in advance, as well as group discounts for teams of three or more. Contact our support team for more details on group pricing.

Can I attend the event remotely?

Yes, the event is designed to be hybrid. You can attend in person or participate virtually from anywhere. Session replays and materials will be available to all attendees for lifetime access.

Do I need prior knowledge in AI or cybersecurity to attend?

Not at all! The sessions are designed for professionals across various industries and backgrounds. Whether you're just getting started or an experienced leader, you'll gain valuable insights and actionable strategies to apply to your business.

Will there be opportunities for 1:1 mentoring or consulting?

While formal mentoring sessions are not part of the event, you will have plenty of opportunities to interact directly with experts, speakers, and other professionals during the Executive Roundtables and networking sessions. Many of our past attendees have formed valuable partnerships through these interactions.

How do I get the LinkedIn-Certified Participation Badge?

After attending the event and completing any necessary post-event assessments or feedback, you’ll receive your LinkedIn-Certified Participation Badge, which you can proudly showcase on your LinkedIn profile and resume.

Can I transfer my pass to someone else?

Yes, we offer pass transfer options. If you are unable to attend, you can transfer your Professional Pass to a colleague. Please contact our support team for assistance with the transfer process.

What if I have specific questions for the speakers or panelists?

During the event, you’ll have opportunities to ask questions directly to speakers through live Q&A sessions. Additionally, VIP Executive Roundtables and AMAs with industry leaders provide an exclusive setting for more personalized discussions.

Is the event accessible for individuals with disabilities?

Yes, we are committed to making our event accessible for all. If you have any specific accessibility needs or requirements, please let us know in advance, and we will do our best to accommodate them.

What should I bring to the event?

If attending in person, bring your professional curiosity, business cards (if applicable), and any questions you want to ask during sessions. For virtual attendees, make sure to have a stable internet connection and a quiet environment to maximize your learning experience.

What is the duration of the event?

The event spans two full days, with sessions, keynotes, and networking opportunities throughout each day. Exact timing details will be shared closer to the event.
